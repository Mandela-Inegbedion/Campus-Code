About Python Weekend

If you want to learn how websites are built, Python Weekend is a great place to begin. This is a beginner-friendly workshop designed for people who are completely new to coding.

We believe the tech industry should be more open and accessible to more people. This workshop is designed to help you take your first step into programming and begin building practical digital skills.

The workshop will take place on 27th - 28th February, 2026.

The workshop is free of charge, but spaces are limited. Applications close on 4th February, 2026.

Apply for the Workshop

Questions You May Already Have

Everything here is designed to make the workshop approachable, practical, and supportive for first-time learners.

Do I need to know anything about websites or programming?

No. This workshop is designed for beginners. You do not need any prior knowledge. If you have some familiarity with HTML or CSS, you are still welcome to apply.

I am not living in Abuja. Can I still attend?

Of course. If you need financial support to travel to Abuja, let us know and we will do our best to help.

Should I bring my own laptop?

Yes. We recommend bringing your own computer so you can continue working on what you build during and after the workshop.

Do I need to have anything installed on my laptop?

Not necessarily. There will be an installation session where facilitators will help you set up all the software you need for the workshop.

Is food provided?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors, snacks and lunch will be served during the workshop.

Will the workshop still work for absolute beginners?

Yes. The pace is structured for people who are just getting started, and facilitators will guide you through the setup and learning process.
