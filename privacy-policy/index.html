Scope of this Policy

This Policy applies to the personal information we collect through the Code Campus website, software applications, social media channels, emails, submitted forms, advertising served on third-party services, and client services. Collectively, these are referred to as the "Services."

This Privacy Policy does not address, and we are not responsible for, the privacy, information, or other practices of any third parties. This includes any third party operating a website or service linked from our Services. The inclusion of a link does not imply endorsement by Code Campus or its affiliates.

By using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and authorize us to collect and process your personal information in accordance with it. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not provide any personal information to us.

What Types of Information Does Code Campus Collect?

"Personal Information" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. It does not include data where the identifier has been removed.

Identity and Demographic Data

Includes your name, gender, date of birth, login credentials, and similar identifying details.

Contact Information

Includes mailing address, billing address, email address, phone number, mobile number, and social media handles.

Technical Usage Data

Includes IP address, browser details, operating system, device details, and location-related technical information.

Payment Data

Most payments are processed by third-party payment providers, though limited payment information may be collected in some cases.

Performance Data

Includes academic progress and performance-tracking information related to Code Campus educational programs.

Marketing and Communications Data

Includes preferences, browsing activity, demographic information, feedback, survey responses, and communication choices.

Anonymized Data

We may also collect, aggregate, and process anonymous or anonymized data for lawful business purposes such as analytics, service improvement, audience insights, and marketing effectiveness. If anonymized data is combined with personal information in a way that makes identification possible, we treat the combined data as personal information.

How Does Code Campus Collect Personal Information?

We directly collect personal information from you when you:

  • Create an account or register to use our Services.
  • Apply to attend educational programs or other Services.
  • Register for events or workshops organized by us or our partners.
  • Respond to surveys, promotional messages, or event invitations.

We use personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected, including to:

Code Campus does not share personal information except in limited situations such as the following:

  • Affiliates and service providers: We may share information with third-party providers that support services such as hosting, analytics, IT infrastructure, customer service, communications, auditing, and event operations.
  • Government, regulatory, and law enforcement bodies: We may disclose information where required by law, legal process, regulation, or where necessary to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Social media and public communication platforms: Information you choose to post publicly may become visible to other users and the public, and will be governed by the privacy practices of those services.
  • Merger, sale, or asset transfer: Personal information may be transferred if Code Campus is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, financing, bankruptcy, or sale of assets.
  • With your consent: We may share information where you expressly agree, such as for co-sponsored events or other activities outside this Policy.

We use physical, technical, and administrative safeguards designed to protect personal information against accidental loss, unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or misuse. Payment transactions are handled through PCI-compliant payment providers.

  • The nature and duration of our relationship with you.
